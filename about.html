Our Story

It started withOne Chair and a Standard

Karil began the way most good salons do — one pair of hands, a lot of patience, and clients who kept coming back because their braids held, their edges survived and nobody made them feel rushed.

Today the lounge covers everything: hair, nails, lashes, brows, make-up, skin and piercing. What has not changed is the rule we started with — if it is not good enough to photograph, it is not good enough to leave the chair.

The Journey

Growing,One Client at a Time

The Beginning

Braids, word of mouth, weekends

Started with braiding and protective styles for friends and family. Every new client arrived because someone else refused to stop talking about their hair.

Growing

Nails and lashes join the chair

Clients kept asking for everything in one sitting. We trained up, invested in proper tools, and stopped sending people elsewhere for their nails and lashes.

The Lounge

A real space, built for comfort

We moved into a proper lounge — good lighting, clean stations, air conditioning and somewhere you can genuinely relax while your set is being done.

Today

Twelve services, one roof

Hair, nails, lashes, brows, make-up, facials and piercing — plus bridal parties who book the whole lounge for the morning of the wedding.

What We Stand For

Our promise,In Four Commitments

Hygiene, Always

Tools sterilised between every client. Single-use needles for piercing. No shortcuts, ever — your safety is not negotiable.

Your Time Respected

We book realistic slots so you are seen when you are booked — and never left waiting three hours for a two-hour appointment.

Honest Pricing

The price we quote is the price you pay. No surprise add-ons at the counter once the work is already done.

Skill You Can See

Every stylist here is trained and keeps training. Trends change constantly — your results should not depend on luck.
